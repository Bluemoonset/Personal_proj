Service

Michael E. DeBakey VA Medical Center

I have volunteered 156 hours total at the hospital's physical therapy department. There, I ran errands, cared for patients,
and performed regular maintanence checks on physical therapy equipment. Volunteering there was easily one of my favorite places to volunteer;
I really felt like I was making a difference. I would recommend it to anyone who wants to broaden their horizons
and meet people from all walks of life!
https://www.va.gov/

First Colony Library

I have volunteered over 70 hours at my local library when I am at home. There, I got to know the staff very well while greeting/helping patrons,
organizing shelves, and performing visitor counts. I've loved books for as long as I can remember, so volunteering here was a joy.
While its not for everyone, being able to help others who are in the shoes of my younger self is something that I hope I can continue to do.
